What to Expect During a Checkup

Learn about the steps involved in a routine dental checkup. This article provides insights into what happens during your visit, from initial assessments to professional cleanings and personalized advice.

A dental checkup is an important part of maintaining oral health. Here’s what you can expect during your visit:

1. Initial Examination

Your dentist will examine your teeth, gums, and mouth to check for any signs of issues such as cavities, gum disease, or oral cancer.

2. Professional Cleaning

A dental hygienist will clean your teeth, removing plaque and tartar buildup. This helps prevent cavities and gum disease.

3. X-Rays

If necessary, your dentist may take X-rays to get a closer look at your teeth and jaw. This helps identify problems that aren’t visible during the examination.

4. Discussion and Recommendations

Your dentist will discuss their findings and recommend any necessary treatments or follow-up appointments.

Regular checkups are essential for maintaining a healthy smile. Don’t hesitate to ask your dentist any questions during your visit!
